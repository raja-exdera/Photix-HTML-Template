About Us

Where Creativity Meets Passion
In Visual Storytelling

At Dot Colours, we help you turn your creativity into a professional skill. Dot Colours Institute provides specialized courses in videography, photography, video editing and photo editing, both for beginners and future professionals who wish to establish a strong foundation in the visual arts field.


In the Photography course, students learn to take creative shots through practical exercise and professional instruction. The videography course is dedicated to learning the art of filming top-notch videos with a great storytelling mindset for every student. For those interested in post-production work, the Video Editing course provides skills in technical knowledge to edit raw video footage into a compelling creative video format, while the Photo Editing program teaches advanced tools and techniques for fine-tuning images with precision and creativity.


Most students from Bangalore and surrounding regions select Dot Colours only because we have a hands-on training method and an innovative atmosphere. We offer step-by-step instruction methods, practical projects and individual feedback to enable learners to develop as confident creators. If you wish to pursue a career in the creative field or just want to hone your skills, Dot Colours is the ideal destination to start your creative journey in photography, videography, and editing.

How We Teach

How We Educate At Dot Colours

We believe in a complete way of learning that enables our students to grow into confident content creators. Our advanced teaching approach is founded on four key steps:

Comprehensive Curriculum

We plan our courses with a solid and well-structured course curriculum that includes the fundamentals, expert-level techniques and practical application of photography, videography and design. Each course is carefully constructed to cater to varying levels of students, so whether a student is a beginner level experience or has already had some training experience, they can gain a solid foundation and continue to develop.

Hands-On Learning

Our teaching style places a higher value on practice rather than the theoretical method of learning. Students work on actual projects, workshops and activities where they implement what they have learned in actual situations. This form of learning is easier to remember, more engaging and provides students with the confidence to implement their skills in the future.

Personalized Support

We maintain a limited batch size so that each student receives individual support from our teachers side. Our instructors provide clear cut feedback and step-by-step guidance according to each learner's needs. This enables students to solve their problems own, learn quickly and progress at their own pace without any other support.

Collaborative Environment

We are convinced that learning gains power when learners study together. That is why we build a warm and welcoming environment where learners exchange their thoughts, ask questions and collaborate as team members. Group conversation, peer assessment and collective projects enable students to view things from a fresh and different perspective and develop creativity while they enjoy the learning experience.

Everything You Want to Know Before You Join here

What makes Dot Colours different from others?

We follow a 100% practical teaching process with limited batch members, tailored feedback and professional-level equipment. We concentrate on real-world skills that prepare students for careers in the creative industry field.

Do I need to bring my own camera gadget or laptop?

Not at all. We offer access to cameras, editing systems and studio setups throughout the course. But if you have your own equipment, we definitely encourage you to practice with that too.

Are the classes online or offline?

For now, our core courses are offline in Bangalore to provide students with the best possible practical learning experience.

Do you offer certificates?

Yes, upon every successful completion of the courses, students are awarded a professionally verified certificate that acknowledges their training and skills.

How do I apply for a course?

You may apply by contacting us personally through our dotcolours website, telephone or our WhatsApp number you can contact. Our team will assist you with the full enrollment process.

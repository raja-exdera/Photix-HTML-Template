The Process of Mastering Photography Editing

Our Photography Editing course is custom-designed to be a thorough and detailed as the learning process of converting your photos to compelling visual narrative stories. This, to be precise, three different modules of learning will hence equip you with all the skills for editing mastery in photography.

Through expert photo editing instructors, every step is intended to provide you with direct hands-on experience working with software such as Lightroom and Photoshop. 1

Software Mastery

It opens with an advanced session introducing industry-standard editing software, like Adobe Lightroom and Photoshop, where you discover how to navigate the layout, work with all the necessary tools, and develop a comprehensive understanding of the editing process.

Hands-On Editing Projects

Amongst the course, you will do a series of hands-on editing projects which cover all other photographic styles such as portrait and landscape, product, showing you different ways in which you can practically apply what you have learned from the class to real-world scenarios as you enhance your eye for detail and increase your creativity.

Portfolio development

You would be given, throughout the course, constructive feedback from experienced instructors over your edited images, learning how to edit properly and making the right choices on your work. You would also be working to create a professional portfolio, featuring the best of your edited images, but also preparing you for future career opportunities as a photographer, so that you would exit the course with a more tangible representation of your abilities.

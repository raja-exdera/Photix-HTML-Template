Frequently Asked Questions

Dot Colours

Before You Enroll

Most students wonder what courses we offer and whether they need any experience beforehand. Dotcolours has options, like photography, videography, and editing courses, which are designed for everyone, whether you are a beginner or you have some experience already. There is no need to have any background to come in.

The course length is also flexible, between 1 month and 6 months, depending on which course you choose. Another question many have is about equipment. “Do I need to purchase my own camera?” You don’t have to, but it’s always good to have one.

We also permit students to cross-list courses, such as Photography and Editing, if you prefer a more holistic learning experience. We offer demo classes to help you decide before you sign up.

After You Enroll

Once you become a member of Dotcolours, you may think about certificates, support, and career choices and placement or job security.

Placements are also provided by us, as we connect students with studios, agencies, and freelance work. Throughout the course, our trainers will always be there to remove all doubts and assist you with personal guidance.

We assist you in developing your portfolio, learning freelancing, and presenting your work to clients.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get clear answers to the most common questions about our courses

How long does each course take to complete?

+

In Dotcolours courses duration usually of 1 month to 6 months, depending on the subject and depth you choose.

Do you provide one-on-one guidance or only group classes?

+

We limit batch sizes to facilitate personal attention from trainers. One-on-one coaching is always on hand when required.

What makes Dotcolours different from other training institutes?

+

Our courses are centered around hands-on practice, industry-standard knowledge, and career guidance rather than theory.

What if I don’t have a creative background?

+

No problem at all. Most of our students join with no experience, and we train them step by step.
